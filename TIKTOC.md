# Computational Finance with AI
## Compact Tik TOC Architecture

**Book type:** MBA / technical finance textbook  
**Status:** Retrospective architecture for developed content  

## Core Thesis

This book teaches computational finance as the use of code, data, models, and AI to reason about valuation, risk, portfolios, derivatives, and financial decision-making.

## Primary Reader

MBA students, finance students, analysts, and technically curious managers who need practical computational fluency.

## Learning Arc

1. **Finance and computation foundations:** returns, risk, data, Python/spreadsheets, and model assumptions.
2. **Core models:** valuation, portfolio optimization, factor models, simulation, and derivatives.
3. **AI-supported finance practice:** forecasting limits, model validation, reporting, and risk governance.

## Chapter Architecture

Each chapter should pair a finance concept with a computational artifact and a model-risk discussion.

## Terminal Capability

The reader can build, interpret, and critique basic computational finance models with appropriate skepticism.

## Adoption Risks

- Finance models can look more precise than they are.
- AI-generated code/calculations require validation.
- Market examples age quickly.
